## Getting in
1. Go to **app.pagescms.org** (bookmark it!).
2. Click **Sign in** and use the link in the email invite Carson sent you.
3. Click your website's name — **you're in.**

## The 4 things you'll do most

### ☎️ Change your phone or email
Left menu → **Contact Details (footer)** → edit the box → **green Save button**.
🎥 [link]

### 📷 Swap the photo at the top
Left menu → **Home Page (top of site)** → first box, **Your photo** → **Upload** → pick your
photo → **Save**.
🎥 [link]

### 🛍️ Change a monthly pick
Left menu → **This Season's Picks** → click a pick → edit the name & description → **Save**.
🎥 [link]

### ➕ Add a new pick
Left menu → **This Season's Picks** → **+ Add** → fill it in → **Save**.
🎥 [link]

---

## Three things to remember
1. **Always click the green Save button** when you're done — that's what publishes it.
2. **Your site updates about a minute after you save.** Refresh the page to see it.
3. **You can't break it.** Every save can be undone, so click around with confidence.
