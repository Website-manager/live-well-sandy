I'm Sandy — mother of ten, grandmother of more than I can count on both hands, a musician and a lifelong learner. For years I've been the friend people call when they want to live a little more naturally and don't know where to begin.

No pressure, no perfect routines. Just small, plant-based choices, shared the way a neighbor shares the good stuff over a cup of tea.
