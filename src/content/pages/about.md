My journey into natural wellness started with a simple, ordinary moment. I was out shopping for homeschool books one day when someone offered to share a few essential oils with me. I was curious, I tried them, and that little moment opened up a whole new world I've loved ever since.

The more I learned, the more it felt like coming home. I'm of Cherokee heritage, and I've always felt a deep connection to my ancestors, who lived close to the land and treasured the plants around them. Exploring plant-based wellness feels like picking up a thread that's been part of my family for generations.

These days, natural products are simply part of how my family lives. My husband and I have raised ten children and now have twenty-two grandchildren, so our home is full, lively, and never quiet for long. I'm a musician at heart — I love to sing, and I direct choirs, orchestras, and bands. When I'm not at home, you'll often find me traveling across the country, and up into Canada, to spend time with people I care about.

What I love most about this work is the people. I'm not here to sell you on anything. I'm here to be a friend and a guide as you explore, to share what I personally enjoy, and to answer your questions along the way. I hold online classes twice a month and a live workshop in my home each month, which I usually record and share so no one misses out. If you've ever been curious about natural wellness, there's room for you here, and you're welcome to learn right alongside me.
